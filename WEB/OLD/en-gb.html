ABOUT US

DLL-Transports Ltd. is engaged in international freight transport. We transport cargo across the European Union, from Latvia to Germany, Holland, Belgium, France, Austria, Italy and Scandinavian countries. At the moment, we have twelve trucks at our disposal, including two megatrailers. From European countries to Latvia we offer composite transport and can also transport dangerous goods (ADR). Our company has high expectations for precise and perfectly executed work and attitude to it.

Company mission:
To enable our customers to focus on growing their business, saving time and resources by providing efficient transport solutions. To be a reliable and convenient logistics partner that can be counted on, whatever the shipment.

Company vision:
To be a leader in the most efficient logistics solutions, setting standards in customer service and innovative solutions, without losing focus on our value - To always go the extra mile for our customers.

Values:
Punctuality
Competence
Quality
